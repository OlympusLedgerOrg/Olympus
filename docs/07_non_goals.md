# Non-Goals

This document explicitly states what Olympus does NOT attempt to do.

## Explicitly Out of Scope

### Not a Blockchain

Olympus is not a blockchain. It does not use:
- Proof of work
- Mining
- Cryptocurrency
- Smart contracts
- Decentralized governance tokens

### Not Access Control

Olympus does not:
- Control who can submit documents
- Enforce permissions on viewing
- Manage user authentication
- Provide encryption of documents

### Not Policy Enforcement

Olympus does not:
- Decide what should be redacted
- Enforce retention policies
- Determine document classification
- Validate legal compliance

### Not Completeness Guarantee

Olympus does not:
- Guarantee all documents are submitted
- Prevent selective withholding
- Force disclosure
- Audit for missing records

## What Olympus IS

Olympus is a cryptographic integrity primitive. It provides tamper evidence and verifiable provenance, but it does not replace legal frameworks, access control systems, or governance processes.

It makes tampering obvious, but it does not prevent tampering.
It proves what was committed, but it does not prove what was omitted.
